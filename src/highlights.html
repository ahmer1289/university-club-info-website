Club Achievements & Highlights

Celebrating the remarkable accomplishments of our student clubs and their dedicated members.

Showcasing Excellence

Our student clubs have achieved remarkable success in competitions, events, and community impact. Here are some of our proudest moments.

National Coding Championship Winners

The Programming Club secured first place in the National Coding Championship 2025, demonstrating exceptional problem-solving skills and teamwork.

Environmental Impact Award

The Green Initiative Club organized a campus-wide recycling drive that diverted over 10 tons of waste from landfills, earning them the University Environmental Impact Award.

Debate Team Triumph

Our Debate Society won the Inter-University Debate Championship, with team member Sarah Khan being named Best Speaker for her compelling arguments on climate policy.

Art Exhibition Success

The Fine Arts Club's annual exhibition attracted over 2,000 visitors and featured works that were later displayed in the National Art Gallery.

Sports Championship Victory

The Cricket Club brought home the trophy from the All-Pakistan University Cricket Tournament, marking the first time our university has won this prestigious title.

Community Service Recognition

The Volunteer Club's literacy program for underprivileged children received the President's Volunteer Service Award for impacting over 500 young lives.
