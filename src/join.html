How to Join a Club

  1. Explore Clubs: Browse the Club Categories page to find clubs that match your interests.
  2. Register Online: Fill out the registration form below with your details and club preference.
  3. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email from the club coordinator with further instructions.
  4. Attend Meetings: Join the club’s first meeting as per the schedule provided in the email. Meeting schedules are also posted on the Upcoming Events page.
  5. Participate: Get involved in club activities, events, and discussions. Regular participation is encouraged but not mandatory.
  6. Stay Updated: Follow club announcements via email or the Achievements page for highlights and important updates.

Complete the form and our club coordinators will contact you with next steps. You can join multiple clubs and participate in their events, meetings, and activities.

Need Help?
